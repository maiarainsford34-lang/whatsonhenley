A Salute to Satchmo

📅 Sunday 27 September 2026 🕖 3:00pm 🚪 Boarding 2:30pm
About the artist

Trumpet star Enrico Tomasso leads an afternoon cruise celebrating the life and music of Louis Armstrong. A former protégé of the great man himself, Enrico met Louis Armstrong aged 7 in 1968 and has dedicated his career to keeping this incredible music alive.

Five-time winner of the British Jazz Award for best trumpeter.
