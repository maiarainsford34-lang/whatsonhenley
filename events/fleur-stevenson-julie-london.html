Fleur Stevenson: A Tribute to Julie London

📅 Sunday 27 September 2026 🎵 2:00pm 🚪 Gates open 12:00pm
About the event

One of the UK's most captivating jazz vocalists, Fleur Stevenson possesses a voice that critics have described as having "power — yet the control to drop down to a caress in a heartbeat." Gifted with a honeyed tone, an offbeat sense of humour and a naturally charismatic stage presence, Fleur creates an instant rapport with her audience that is simply irresistible.

For this very special afternoon, Fleur turns her artistry to the timeless songs of Julie London — Cry Me a River, Blue Moon, Fly Me to the Moon, I'm in the Mood for Love and more — in a touching tribute on what would have been Julie's 100th birthday. Set against the stunning backdrop of Oaken Grove Vineyard, with fine wine and stone-baked pizza, this is an afternoon not to be missed.
