Riverboat Shuffle

📅 Saturday 26 September 2026 🕖 3:00pm 🚪 Boarding 2:30pm
About the artist

Clarinetist Adrian Cox leads an afternoon cruise aboard Henley's Mississippi-style paddle steamer playing authentic New Orleans jazz — King Oliver, Jelly Roll Morton, Sidney Bechet and more.

Originally from Sussex, now resident in Sweden, Adrian is recognised as one of the finest jazz clarinettists in the world.

"Indisputably one of the best clarinettists in the world!" — Jamie Cullum
